    A Music Festival Supporting the Animal Protective Foundation

    Sunday, June 11th, 2023 | 12pm - 5pm

     

    Buy Tickets

    The Animal Protective Foundation is sponsoring a brand-new music festival this summer: Woofstock!

    Woofstock is a family- and dog-friendly event that will feature live music from Twenty Ninety-Six, a chicken BBQ dinner, food trucks, craft beer and wine, and a variety of other specialty vendors.

    Woofstock will also feature a kid’s zone with activities for all ages, so grab the whole family—yes, even Fido—and head down to Mohawk Harbor in Schenectady on Sunday, June 11th for this awesome event. Lawn chairs or blankets are encouraged!

    *All proceeds from the event will benefit the animals at the Animal Protective Foundation.*

    About Animal Protective Foundation

    The Animal Protective Foundation is a humane society providing services to Schenectady County and the greater Capital Region. Since 1931, the APF has held true to the mission of its founders. We have an open admission policy which ensures that animals are not turned away based on their breed, age, condition, or circumstance.
